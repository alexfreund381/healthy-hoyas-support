Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my meals and track nutrition?
Tap the "Food Log" tab, then use "+ Add Meal" to search for foods using our USDA database. You can log breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks with detailed nutritional information.
Can I change my nutrition goals after setting them up?
Yes! Go to the "Settings" tab and update your calorie goals, macro percentages, and micronutrient targets. Changes will apply immediately to your daily tracking.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can permanently delete your account from the "Settings" tab. This will remove all your data including meal logs, weight tracking, and profile information from our servers.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • App not loading? Try closing and reopening the app, or restart your device if the issue persists.
  • Food search not working? Check your internet connection. The USDA food database requires an active connection.
  • Data not syncing? Make sure you're signed in to your account and have a stable internet connection.
  • Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen to reset it via email.
