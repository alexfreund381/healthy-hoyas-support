Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log meals from Georgetown dining halls?
Healthy Hoyas is integrated with Georgetown's dining services! You can easily log meals from Leo's, Epicurean, and other campus dining locations. The app automatically pulls nutritional data for dining hall items, making it seamless to track your on-campus meals.
Pro tip: You can also search our comprehensive USDA and OpenFoodFacts databases for off-campus meals and groceries.
What nutritional information can I track?
Track both macronutrients (calories, protein, carbs, fat) and micronutrients (vitamins, minerals, sodium, fiber, and more). Set personalized goals for each nutrient based on your dietary needs, activity level, and health objectives. The app provides detailed breakdowns and progress tracking for comprehensive nutritional awareness.
Custom Goals: Adjust calorie targets, macro percentages, and micronutrient limits in Settings.
How does the activity and wellness tracking work?
Beyond nutrition, Healthy Hoyas helps you track your complete wellness picture:
  • Steps: Log daily step counts and activity quality
  • Sleep: Track sleep duration and quality ratings
  • Weight: Monitor weight changes over time with goal tracking
  • Analysis: View comprehensive charts showing trends, correlations, and progress across all health metrics
Can I manually add foods not in the database?
Absolutely! While we have extensive USDA and OpenFoodFacts databases plus Georgetown dining integration, you can manually enter custom foods with their nutritional information. This ensures you can log everything you eat, from homemade recipes to specialty items not in our databases.
How do I interpret the analysis charts and data?
The Analysis tab provides powerful insights into your health patterns:
  • Weekly and monthly nutrition trends
  • Activity vs. nutrition correlations
  • Goal achievement tracking across all metrics
  • Sleep and weight pattern analysis
Use these insights to make informed decisions about your diet, exercise, and lifestyle habits.
How do I change my nutrition and fitness goals?
Go to the "Settings" tab to update your goals anytime:
  • Calorie targets based on your activity level and goals
  • Macro percentage splits (protein, carbs, fat)
  • Micronutrient limits (sodium, sugar, vitamins, minerals)
  • Weight goals and target timeline
  • Activity and sleep targets
Changes apply immediately to your daily tracking and progress calculations.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Georgetown dining data not loading? Check your internet connection. Campus dining integration requires network access to pull the latest menu information.
  • Food search not finding items? Try searching with different terms or check spelling. You can search our USDA database, OpenFoodFacts, or switch to manual entry if needed.
  • Charts not displaying properly? Make sure you have logged data for the time period you're viewing. Analysis requires at least a few days of tracked information.
  • Data not syncing between sessions? Ensure you're signed in to your account and have a stable internet connection. Your data is securely stored in the cloud.
  • Goal calculations seem off? Double-check your personal information (age, weight, activity level) in Settings, as these affect calorie and nutrient recommendations.
  • App running slowly? Try closing other apps or restarting your device. Large amounts of historical data may occasionally impact performance.
